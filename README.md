SDG 13: Climate Action Hackathon 2025 Website
  Project Overview

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 website is a promotional and informational platform for an upcoming event focused on urban heat and flood resilience, aligned with United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 13 – Climate Action.
The site highlights event details, challenges, speakers, and registration options to encourage participation and raise awareness about climate innovation.

  Event Details

Event: Climate Action Hackathon 2025
Theme: Urban Heat & Flood Resilience
Dates: March 14–16, 2025
Location: Nairobi Innovation Hub
Goal: Inspire changemakers, developers, and students to create digital solutions addressing climate-related challenges.

  Features

  Hero Section – Engaging introduction with call-to-action “Register Now” button.

  About Section – Overview of the hackathon’s mission and connection to SDG 13.

  Climate Mitigation Solutions – Highlights renewable energy, efficiency, and electrification.

  Speakers & Mentors – Showcases experts and thought leaders.

  SDG Challenges – Describes real-world innovation challenges aligned with global goals.

  Registration Form – Allows participants to sign up easily.

  Event Proceedings – Displays workshops, panels, and other program highlights.

  Footer Section – Provides contact info and links to official SDG resources.
  
  Design & Styling

Color Palette:

Primary: #004708 (deep green – sustainability and nature)

Accent: #049624, #2e8b57, #85dd85 (vibrant greens – energy and growth)

Text: White on dark background for contrast and readability

Typography:

Font Family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif

Consistent sizing and spacing for accessibility and visual clarity

Layout:

CSS Grid for responsive sections (speakers, challenges, and solutions)

Flexbox used in the hero and form layouts

Fully responsive design for mobile and tablet screens
