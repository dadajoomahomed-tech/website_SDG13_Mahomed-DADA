About the Hackathon

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025, held in Nairobi at the Innovation Hub from March 14-16, brings together passionate participants to co-create digital solutions that help communities adapt to rising heat and flood risks in cities. The event supports SDG 13: Climate Action through innovation and collaboration.

Climate Mitigation Solutions

These solutions reduce greenhouse gas emissions, helping slow global warming and build a cleaner, safer future.

Renewable Energy

Clean power sources like solar, wind, and hydro replace fossil fuels, cutting CO₂ emissions and promoting sustainable development.

Energy Efficiency

Using energy more efficiently in homes, transport, and industry lowers emissions and saves money while improving comfort and productivity.

Electrification of Transport & Heating

Switching to electric vehicles and heat pumps eliminates fossil fuel use in daily life, cutting air pollution and carbon emissions.

SDG Challenges

Our event challenges participants to think critically and act boldly. Each challenge focuses on solving real-world issues aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Climate Innovation Challenge

Design creative solutions that help reduce carbon emissions or promote environmental restoration — from green energy systems to reforestation initiatives.

Goal: Support SDG 13 — Climate Action

Education for All Challenge

Develop ideas or digital tools that make learning accessible for all, especially in underprivileged communities or remote areas.

Goal: Support SDG 4 — Quality Education

Clean Energy Challenge

Build sustainable energy projects or awareness campaigns that encourage renewable power use in everyday life.

Goal: Support SDG 7 — Affordable & Clean Energy
